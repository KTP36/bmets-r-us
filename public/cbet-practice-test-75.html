Sample CBET Practice Questions

1. What device is used to measure oxygen saturation?
A. ECG
B. Pulse oximeter
C. Ventilator
D. Infusion pump
Answer: B

2. What does a defibrillator do?
A. Monitors heart rhythm
B. Delivers a shock to restore normal rhythm
C. Measures blood pressure
D. Delivers oxygen
Answer: B

3. What does Ohm’s Law state?
A. V = IR
B. P = IV
C. E = mc²
D. F = ma
Answer: A
