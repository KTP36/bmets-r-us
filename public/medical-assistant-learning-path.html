About This Path

The Medical Assistant Learning Path is a supplemental educational resource designed to reinforce concepts commonly taught in medical assistant programs. It is not an accredited program and does not replace formal education, clinical training, certification requirements, employer policies, or professional medical guidance. Completing modules or earning achievements does not confer certification, licensure, continuing education credit, or an accredited credential.
