How to Use Equipment ID Practice Effectively

1. Do a first pass quickly

Answer based on current recall to expose weak areas.

2. Review misses right away

Immediate review helps lock in distinctions between similar devices.

3. Repeat until consistent

Focus on correct identification speed and confidence, not just one good attempt.

4. Pair with CBET practice questions

Use equipment recognition and question-based reasoning together for stronger overall prep.
