Lungs Anatomy Practice Questions

  1. How many lobes does the right lung have?
    • A. One
    • B. Two
    • C. Three
    • D. Four
    Answer: C. Three
  2. How many lobes does the left lung have?
    • A. One
    • B. Two
    • C. Three
    • D. Four
    Answer: B. Two
  3. Which structure carries air from the trachea into the lungs?
    • A. Esophagus
    • B. Bronchi
    • C. Alveoli
    • D. Pleura
    Answer: B. Bronchi
  4. Gas exchange occurs in the:
    • A. Bronchi
    • B. Trachea
    • C. Diaphragm
    • D. Alveoli
    Answer: D. Alveoli
  5. Which muscle plays a major role in breathing?
    • A. Deltoid
    • B. Diaphragm
    • C. Biceps
    • D. Trapezius
    Answer: B. Diaphragm
  6. The lungs are covered by which membrane?
    • A. Pericardium
    • B. Peritoneum
    • C. Pleura
    • D. Endocardium
    Answer: C. Pleura
  7. Which lung contains the cardiac notch?
    • A. Right lung
    • B. Left lung
    • C. Both lungs
    • D. Neither lung
    Answer: B. Left lung
  8. The trachea divides into the right and left:
    • A. Alveoli
    • B. Bronchi
    • C. Lobes
    • D. Pleural sacs
    Answer: B. Bronchi
