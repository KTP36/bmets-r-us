Medical Prefix & Suffix Guide

Learn how to break down medical terms using prefixes and suffixes.

Why Prefixes and Suffixes Matter

Medical terms can seem complicated, but most are built from smaller parts. By understanding prefixes and suffixes, you can quickly figure out what a word means.

Common Medical Prefixes

PrefixMeaningExample

Tachy-FastTachycardia

Common Medical Suffixes

SuffixMeaningExample
-itisInflammationArthritis
-ectomyRemovalAppendectomy
-ologyStudy ofBiology
-algiaPainNeuralgia
-emiaBlood conditionAnemia

How to Break Down Terms

  • Hypertension → Hyper (high) + tension (pressure)
  • Bradycardia → Brady (slow) + cardia (heart)
  • Appendectomy → Appendix + ectomy (removal)
