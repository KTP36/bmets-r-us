What is the Heart?

The heart is a muscular organ that pumps blood throughout the body. It delivers oxygen and nutrients while removing waste products.

Heart Chambers

The heart has four chambers:

  • Right Atrium
  • Right Ventricle
  • Left Atrium
  • Left Ventricle

The right side handles deoxygenated blood, while the left side pumps oxygenated blood to the body.

Heart Valves

The heart contains four main valves:

  • Tricuspid Valve
  • Pulmonary Valve
  • Mitral Valve
  • Aortic Valve

These valves ensure blood flows in the correct direction.

Blood Flow Through the Heart

Blood flows in this order:

  1. Body → Right Atrium
  2. Right Ventricle → Lungs
  3. Lungs → Left Atrium
  4. Left Ventricle → Body
