What is the CBET Exam?

The CBET certification is administered by the Association for the Advancement of Medical Instrumentation (AAMI). It validates the knowledge and skills of biomedical equipment technicians who maintain, repair, and operate medical devices in hospitals, clinics, and healthcare facilities.

Key Topics Covered:

  • Electrical Safety & Device Fundamentals
  • Patient Monitoring Systems
  • Therapeutic Equipment Operation
  • Ventilators & Respiratory Systems
  • Cardiac & Hemodynamic Devices
  • Quality Control & Maintenance
  • Troubleshooting & Repair Procedures

CBET Exam Tips & Study Strategy

  • Start Early: Begin studying at least 8-12 weeks before your exam date.
  • Use Practice Questions: Spend 60-70% of study time on practice questions to identify weak areas.
  • Review Missed Questions: Analyze every mistake to understand the underlying concept.
  • Study in Context: Learn how equipment functions in real clinical scenarios.
  • Take Timed Quizzes: Practice under timed conditions to build speed and accuracy.
