Test your understanding of heart anatomy with free practice questions covering chambers, valves, blood flow, and major cardiovascular structures.

Heart Anatomy Practice Questions

  1. Which chamber receives deoxygenated blood from the body?
    • A. Left atrium
    • B. Right atrium
    • C. Left ventricle
    • D. Right ventricle
    Answer: B. Right atrium
  2. Which valve separates the left atrium from the left ventricle?
    • A. Tricuspid valve
    • B. Pulmonary valve
    • C. Mitral valve
    • D. Aortic valve
    Answer: C. Mitral valve
  3. Which vessel carries oxygenated blood away from the heart to the body?
    • A. Pulmonary artery
    • B. Superior vena cava
    • C. Aorta
    • D. Coronary sinus
    Answer: C. Aorta
  4. The pulmonary artery carries blood from the heart to which organ?
    • A. Liver
    • B. Brain
    • C. Kidneys
    • D. Lungs
    Answer: D. Lungs
  5. Which chamber pumps oxygenated blood into the systemic circulation?
    • A. Right atrium
    • B. Right ventricle
    • C. Left atrium
    • D. Left ventricle
    Answer: D. Left ventricle
  6. Which valve prevents backflow from the right ventricle into the right atrium?
    • A. Mitral valve
    • B. Tricuspid valve
    • C. Aortic valve
    • D. Pulmonary valve
    Answer: B. Tricuspid valve
  7. Oxygenated blood returns from the lungs to the heart through which vessels?
    • A. Pulmonary veins
    • B. Pulmonary arteries
    • C. Superior vena cava
    • D. Coronary arteries
    Answer: A. Pulmonary veins
  8. Which structure separates the right and left sides of the heart?
    • A. Pericardium
    • B. Septum
    • C. Myocardium
    • D. Endocardium
    Answer: B. Septum

Why Practice Heart Anatomy?

Heart anatomy is a core topic for students preparing for anatomy exams, nursing exams, TEAS-related science review, and medical terminology study. Repetition helps build speed, confidence, and better recall.
