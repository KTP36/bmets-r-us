Foot Anatomy Quiz

  1. The heel bone is called:
    • A. Talus
    • B. Calcaneus
    • C. Navicular
    • D. Cuboid
    Answer: B — The calcaneus forms the heel.
  2. How many toes bones (phalanges) are there?
    • A. 10
    • B. 12
    • C. 14
    • D. 16
    Answer: C — There are 14 phalanges in the foot.
  3. The ankle bone is called:
    • A. Talus
    • B. Fibula
    • C. Tibia
    • D. Calcaneus
    Answer: A — The talus forms part of the ankle joint.
