Skull Anatomy Quiz

  1. Which bone forms the forehead?
    • A. Parietal
    • B. Frontal
    • C. Temporal
    • D. Occipital
    Answer: B — The frontal bone forms the forehead.
  2. Which bone houses the ear structures?
    • A. Temporal
    • B. Nasal
    • C. Zygomatic
    • D. Maxilla
    Answer: A — The temporal bone contains structures for hearing.
  3. The back of the skull is formed by:
    • A. Frontal
    • B. Occipital
    • C. Parietal
    • D. Mandible
    Answer: B — The occipital bone forms the back of the skull.
