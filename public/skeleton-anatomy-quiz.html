Skeleton Anatomy Practice Questions

  1. Which bone protects the brain?
    • A. Scapula
    • B. Sternum
    • C. Cranium
    • D. Femur
    Answer: C. Cranium
  2. Which bone is commonly called the collarbone?
    • A. Clavicle
    • B. Humerus
    • C. Ulna
    • D. Radius
    Answer: A. Clavicle
  3. Which is the largest bone in the human body?
    • A. Tibia
    • B. Humerus
    • C. Pelvis
    • D. Femur
    Answer: D. Femur
  4. The patella is located in which part of the body?
    • A. Elbow
    • B. Knee
    • C. Wrist
    • D. Shoulder
    Answer: B. Knee
  5. Which bone is the shinbone?
    • A. Fibula
    • B. Tibia
    • C. Femur
    • D. Talus
    Answer: B. Tibia
  6. Which bones form the vertebral column?
    • A. Ribs
    • B. Tarsals
    • C. Vertebrae
    • D. Carpals
    Answer: C. Vertebrae
  7. Which bone is located in the upper arm?
    • A. Radius
    • B. Ulna
    • C. Humerus
    • D. Clavicle
    Answer: C. Humerus
  8. The scapula is also known as the:
    • A. Shoulder blade
    • B. Hip bone
    • C. Breastbone
    • D. Jawbone
    Answer: A. Shoulder blade

Why Practice Skeleton Anatomy?

Learning skeletal anatomy helps with anatomy classes, nursing prep, medical terminology, and faster recognition of common bone structures.
